## 5. Potential Clients Discovery: Sliding Card Interface

### Objective

Provide users with a continuously updated, AI-curated view of potential customers they can immediately target with their business, product, or services.

### UI Placement

* **Primary Location:** Main dashboard body (below the header, above analytics or campaign summaries)
* **Secondary Access:** Click-through from Command Center → Leads Library

### Component: “Potential Clients” Card Slider

**Design Characteristics:**

* Horizontally scrollable slideshow of smart cards
* Auto-scroll with manual swipe/arrow navigation
* Responsive across desktop and mobile views

**Each Card Displays:**

* Client or company name
* Industry / niche
* Location (country / region)
* Estimated company size or customer profile
* AI-generated “Why this is a good fit” insight
* Suggested outreach channel (Email, LinkedIn, Social, Website)
* Action buttons:

  * **Add to Leads Folder**
  * **Start Outreach**
  * **Generate Message**

**AI Intelligence Layer:**

* Cards are dynamically generated based on:

  * User’s product description
  * Previous campaigns
  * Engagement and conversion history
* Continuously refreshed as the system learns user preferences and performance.

**Value:**

* Eliminates guesswork in lead targeting
* Turns discovery into immediate execution
* Encourages proactive growth actions directly from the dashboard

## 6. Discovery & Learning System: Progressive Insight Snap

### Objective

Guide users to fully unlock the platform’s capabilities through contextual education, without overwhelming them.

### UI Placement

* **Top-right corner of the header bar**
* Represented by a **Discovery icon** (e.g., compass or spark icon)

---

### Component: Discovery Progress Snap (20% → 100%)

**Interaction Behavior:**

* Clicking the Discovery icon opens a slide-in or pop-up panel
* Panel visually shows a progress indicator with milestones:

  * 20%
  * 40%
  * 60%
  * 80%
  * 100%

**At Each Level, the Snap Provides:**

* Short, actionable tips
* Platform-specific usage recommendations
* Context-aware tutorials based on user activity
* Curated articles and micro-guides (2–3 minutes read)

**Examples:**

* **20%:**
  “How to describe your product for better AI results”
* **40%:**
  “Linking social accounts to enable one-click publishing”
* **60%:**
  “Using Leads Folders to scale multiple campaigns”
* **80%:**
  “Optimizing outreach with AI-driven personalization”
* **100%:**
  “Advanced autonomous workflows and growth automation”

**Smart Adaptation:**

* Progress advances based on:

  * Feature usage
  * Completed actions (e.g., linking accounts, launching campaigns)
  * Time spent engaging with platform features

**Value:**

* Reduces user churn
* Accelerates time-to-value
* Creates a guided mastery path instead of static documentation
